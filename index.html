About Us

MENd: Supporting Men's Mental Health in Kensington & Chelsea, Hammersmith & Fulham

MENd is a Community Interest Company dedicated to men's mental health through free walk and talk sessions. Founded by a man who's faced his own struggles, we're creating a supportive community for men in Hammersmith and Fulham, Kensington and Chelsea. Our walks encourage open conversations on tough topics like suicide and depression, while enjoying fresh air and exercise. Currently a solo venture, we're growing one step at a time to break the silence and build resilience.

Peer-led walk and talk sessions

Free and confidential support

Weekly walks in local parks

Suicide Awareness in Fulham

Men's suicide rate in the UK is 17.4 per 100,000, three times higher than women. Walks focus on prevention and support.

75-80% of suicides are men

Highest rates in 45-49 age group

Early conversations save lives

Depression Support in Chelsea
